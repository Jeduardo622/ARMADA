# Mission Spec Template

- Mission ID/Name:
- Narrative beat:
- Objectives: primary, secondary (3-star goals).
- Enemy setup: ships, AI profiles (aggressive/kiting/line-advance/boss), formations.
- Environment: wind pattern, obstacles, modifiers.
- Player constraints: allowed ships/captains, turn limit, retries.
- Rewards: gold, mats, shards, cosmetics tokens.
- Tuning knobs: HP multipliers, damage modifiers, AI accuracy, wind variance.
- Telemetry: expected signals (fail reasons taxonomy, turn count, damage profile expectations).
- QA notes: edge cases, repro steps, perf-sensitive areas.
