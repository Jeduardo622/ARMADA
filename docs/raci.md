# RACI (Responsibility Matrix)

Use this to clarify ownership and escalation. “A” has decision authority.

- Product/Strategy: A – scope, roadmap, gates; R – backlog/prioritization.
- Architecture/ADR: A – cross-cutting decisions; R – ADRs, reviews.
- Game Design: A – combat rules/progression; R – mission specs, tuning asks.
- Client/Gameplay Eng: A – client tech/determinism; R – UI/UX, sim, perf.
- Backend/Services Eng: A – backend/API/data integrity; R – economy auth.
- Tech Art/Tools: A – pipelines/asset compliance; R – build tooling/LOD.
- QA/Automation: A – quality gates; R – test plans, harnesses, device runs.
- Live Ops/Analytics: A – flags/experiments; R – dashboards, cohorts.
- Production/PM: A – schedule/RAID; R – ceremonies, dependencies.
- Security/Compliance: A – privacy/store compliance; R – threat model, checks.
- Audio (PT): R – SFX pass; C – mix/perf; I – PM for schedule.

Escalation: Blockers → PM → Product/Architecture for scope/decision → Exec.
